Instructions

Add Annotation

Click and drag to highlight text you would like to add an annotation to. A prompt will appear asking for the label/category. Type in your label/category and click 'OK'

Edit Annotation

Click on the highlighted chapter text. A prompt will appear where you can replace the label/category. Then click 'OK' to save.

Delete Annotation

Click on the highlighted chapter text. A prompt will appear. Delete the current label/category text and then click 'OK' to save. Shortcut: once the category appears press 'delete' and then 'enter' on your keyboard.
